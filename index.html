03

How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect exclusively to provide, operate, and improve the Konnect Marketing platform. Specific uses include:

  • Authenticating users and managing access to platform features
  • Sending and receiving WhatsApp messages via the Meta Cloud API on behalf of business clients
  • Operating the CRM inbox, Kanban pipeline, and contact management features
  • Executing mass dispatch campaigns using approved WhatsApp Business message templates
  • Running automated follow-up workflows and AI agent interactions
  • Generating dispatch reports and delivery analytics
  • Maintaining platform security and preventing unauthorized access
  • Complying with legal obligations under Brazilian law (LGPD) and Meta Platform Terms
We do not sell your data. We do not use personal data for advertising purposes, and we do not share data with third parties for marketing purposes.
04

Meta Platform Data

As a WhatsApp Business Technology Provider, our platform integrates with the Meta WhatsApp Business Cloud API. In this context, we access and process Meta Platform Data on behalf of our business clients.

Permissions used:

  • whatsapp_business_messaging — Used to send and receive WhatsApp messages on behalf of connected business accounts, including mass dispatch, CRM inbox responses, and AI agent interactions
  • whatsapp_business_management — Used to retrieve approved message templates, manage WhatsApp Business Account settings, and synchronize template status for client accounts

All Meta Platform Data is processed strictly in accordance with Meta's Platform Terms, Developer Policies, and the WhatsApp Business Policy. We do not use Meta Platform Data for any purposes beyond those disclosed in this policy and authorized by our business clients.

Data minimization: We only request and store the minimum Meta Platform Data necessary to provide the services described in this policy. Access tokens are stored encrypted and are never exposed to unauthorized parties.

06

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure. We implement the following security measures:

  • All data transmissions use TLS 1.2 or higher encryption
  • Access tokens and credentials are stored encrypted at rest using AES-256
  • Access to production databases is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Authentication uses JWT tokens with configurable expiration
  • Row-level security (RLS) is enforced at the database layer to prevent cross-client data access
  • Regular security audits and dependency updates
While we implement industry-standard security practices, no system is completely immune to security risks. We encourage users to use strong passwords and to report any suspected security incidents immediately.
07

Data Retention

We retain data for the following periods:

  • Account data: For the duration of the active account, plus 90 days after account termination
  • Conversation history and message content: 12 months from the date of the message, unless the client requests earlier deletion
  • Dispatch reports and analytics: 24 months
  • Access logs and technical data: 90 days

Upon account termination, clients may request complete data deletion. We will process such requests within 30 days and provide confirmation of deletion.

08

User Rights (LGPD)

Under the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados — LGPD, Law No. 13.709/2018), you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right of correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right of deletion — request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention obligations
  • Right of portability — request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to information — request information about third parties with whom your data is shared
  • Right to revoke consent — withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Right to object — object to processing of your data for specific purposes

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address provided in Section 12. We will respond to all requests within 15 business days.

Cookies

Our platform uses the following types of cookies and local storage:

  • Authentication cookies: JWT session tokens stored in browser local storage to maintain logged-in state
  • Preference storage: Local storage entries to remember UI preferences such as selected dispatch configurations
  • Analytics: We do not use third-party analytics cookies

These are strictly necessary for platform functionality. You may clear browser storage at any time, which will require you to log in again.
